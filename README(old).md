### **Logo and description:**

The website we're making is about the pinnacle of motorsport, Formula One. Learn about its rich history, from the first ever WDC winner, to the dominating drivers of the modern era. Learn about the different engines used, like the old era V12, to the V6 Hybrid engine, and the parts that make a F1 vehicle what it is. Get informed on the future of the sport with the 2026 car regulations, as well as the new teams joining next season, Cadillac, and Audi, replacing Kick Sauber. 

Get to know the current driver lineup for the 2025 season, with the current standings for the World Driver's Championship, and the winner of the Constructor's Championship, McLaren. Celebrate the drivers who made the sport more popular, and more competitive, like Ayrton Senna, Michael Schumacher, Niki Lauda, Lewis Hamilton, Max Verstappen, and others. Finally, learn the schedule of a Formula One weekend, from Free Practice 1, 2, and, 3 to adjust team strategies, to Qualifying 1, 2, and, 3 to determine the starting grid, and the Grand Prix, the main race where drivers can earn points by finishing in the top 10.

### **Website outline:**

**Home page:** This will be the main hub where you are able to access all the other web pages in a dropdown menu.

**P1(History)** = This webpage will detail the history of F1 racing, from its humble beginnings to what it is today.

**P2(Engine evolution)** = This webpage will detail the development of engines used in F1 racing, highlighting how they continue to push the limits of F1

**P3(The 2026 car regulations)** = This webpage will show the 2026 sporting regulations, currently enforced by the FIA

**P4(Driver Lineup for the 2025 Season)** = This webpage will simply show the lineup of drivers for this year's season, accompanied by a short description for each driver.

**P5(Race Weekend)** = This webpage will detail what goes on during a race weekend and its importance.

**P6(World Driver's Championship standings)** = This webpage will be a leaderboard showing the standings and rankings of each driver for this season.

**P7(Constructor's Championship winner)** = This webpage will show the team that scored the most points during this season.

**P8(Driver Hall of Fame/Legendary Drivers)** = This webpage will highlight the extraordinary feats and records accomplished by certain drivers.

**References:** This page will always be available on the bottom of the website, linking to all the sources used for information and images.
 
---
JS will be incorporated in our website through buttons and interactable ui, leading you through the website and revealing certain elements. Examples of these would be the logo near the title, which would redirect you to the home page and the windows on the home page which would redirect you to other webpages.
